Hello PSUDO World

The Penn State University Development Operations Group (PSUDO) fosters an environment for exploration of infrastructure tools that advance development-operations culture.

We regularly meet and bring ideas, energy, and a desire to participate with code stained hands to learn about Dev Ops tools.

The best way to engage and get started in the fast-moving dev ops environment with us is with asynchronous chat available through our slack workspace at http://psudo-group.slack.com
Those with @psu.edu emails will be approved to join automatically.
